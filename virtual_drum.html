How to play
  1. Play the kit Tap the drums or use keys Q W E R A S L and Space.
  2. Set the volume Use the Master volume slider to adjust loudness.
  3. Record a groove Press Record, play, then press Record again to stop.
  4. Play back Press Play to hear what you recorded.
  5. Loop it Turn on Loop playback and hit Play. Press Stop to end the loop.
  6. Try again Use Clear to erase the pattern and record something new.
